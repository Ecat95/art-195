Glass House

The project lets you interact and see a 3d space . The viewer can use the WASD keys to move around.

PUSH
Artist Statement

In my works there is a theme of poking at reality/everyday life though a fantastical world. The works are currently use blender, and after-effects as the main programs in use. I am interested in continuing and building on the unreality of my works.
The 3D figures are made in blender. The controllable land of the project that holds everything and lets the viewer move is A-Frame. The A-Frame lets everything be laid out for the viewer to look around. The viewer can go left or right from the starting point and see a few of the outdoor dangers in a space.
